# Project Wiki

Welcome to this project's wiki — human-and-agent-readable documentation for this codebase. The four sections below follow the Diátaxis convention ([ADR 0004](https://github.com/alexherrero/agentic-harness/blob/main/wiki/architecture/decisions/0004-diataxis-documentation-spec.md)): each page is written for a single reader intent — learning, doing, looking up, or understanding — and never mixes modes.

> [!NOTE]
> This page is maintained by the `documenter` sub-agent at phase boundaries. Humans may edit it directly; the sub-agent preserves human edits.

## 📚 New here? Learn by doing.

Tutorials walk you through a concrete outcome end-to-end.

- [Getting started](01-Getting-Started) — from a fresh clone to a green test run.

## 🔧 Trying to do something specific?

- [First how-to](First-How-To) — placeholder; replace with a real recipe when one earns its keep.
